About Me

My name is Pieter Flamand but people call me Phylax, that's my artistic name. I'm a regular student who is passionate about everything around music. I've started playing guitar when i was 7 years old, thanks to my uncle who inspired me in everything i managed to learn playing guitar very fast later than I started to sing along the guitar melodies. People started asking me to sing for them and every time I sang, It felt like i was in some magical world outside of our planet. After all these years with music, I'm now 21 years old and i can't imagine my life without music!

A Real Traveler

  • Why i love traveling?

I love the excitement of it all–feeling like an explorer–discovering a new land, a new language, new money and all the little challenges that go along with it. When I travel, I am more spontaneous and don’t live for the future or the past… just in the moment. Being impulsive and saying ‘yes’ to nearly everything is part of the fun.

I'm now 21 and have been in 11 different countries! My goal is to double that value within coming 10 years!

Pieter has been to: Belgium, Egypt, France, Germany, Greece, Ireland, Italy, Luxembourg, Netherlands, Spain, United Kingdom
